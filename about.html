About Us

We are unstoppable enthusiasts who since 2006 have been redefining motorsport in Macedonia through the availability of world brands and trends.

For every fan of this sport we have an offer of top quality products and designs and every year we surprise with an even better and richer selection.

We believe that adrenaline and safety must go hand in hand and that quality tires, parts and protective equipment are everything - but a luxury and we prove it through our affordable prices.

For scooters, bikers, enduro, cross, touring - we are here to equip you for your adventure: from clothes, tires and parts, to knowledge, tips and recommendations!
